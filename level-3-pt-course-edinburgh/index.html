What You'll Learn on the Level 3 PT Course

Our curriculum goes beyond the minimum requirements. Alongside the regulated anatomy and programme design modules, you'll cover business, client psychology, and the commercial realities of being a PT.

01

Anatomy & Physiology

Musculoskeletal system, energy systems, biomechanics, and how the body adapts to training stimulus.

02

Exercise Science & Programming

Principles of training, periodisation, progressive overload, and how to design effective programmes for different goals.

03

Client Assessment & Consultation

PAR-Q, lifestyle questionnaires, postural analysis, fitness testing protocols, and how to translate results into action.

04

Nutrition Principles

Evidence-based nutrition for body composition, performance, and health. How to advise clients within your scope of practice.

05

Professional Practice

Insurance, legal responsibilities, duty of care, professional standards, and CIMSPA Code of Conduct.

06

The Career Blueprint

Client acquisition, pricing strategy, social media for PTs, and how to build a client list from your first week qualified.
