## Tutor Support Calls — 6 × 30 Minutes

### Call 1 — Introduction & Learning Plan
- Course structure overview and module walkthroughs
- Personalised learning plan (based on weekly study hours)
- Review of learner questionnaire
- Confirmation of payment plan if applicable
- Open Q&A

### Call 2 — Level 2 Module Check-In
- Level 2 module progress review
- Level 2 quiz results discussion
- Guidance on selecting your client
- Personalised learner advice
- Open Q&A

### Call 3 — Gym Instructor Assessment Prep
- Full walkthrough of the practical assessment format
- What to expect, how to prepare, and what you will be assessed on
- Open Q&A

### Call 4 — Level 2 Review & Level 3 Intro
- Level 2 reflection and celebration of progress
- Introduction to Level 3 PT modules — structure and expectations
- Open Q&A

### Call 5 — Level 3 Check-In & PT Assessment Prep
- Level 3 module progress and personalised advice
- Level 3 practical assessment walkthrough
- Business and career advice
- Open Q&A

### Call 6 — Final Check-In & Qualification Readiness
- Level 3 final module check-in
- Practical assessment review and sign-off preparation
- Business and career advice — round two
- Final open Q&A

## Level 2 — Gym Instructor Workshops (2 Hours Each)

### Workshop 1 — Gym Instructor: Foundations
- UK Physical Activity Guidelines
- Client consent, health screening and goal setting
- Introduction to fundamental strength movement patterns
- Introduction to cardiovascular training and RPE
- Pre-requisite modules: Anatomy & Physiology, Maximise the Customer Experience, Routine Maintenance & Cleaning, Professional Practice, Consultation, Lifestyle Management & Risk Stratification

### Workshop 2 — Gym Instructor: Practical Assessment Prep
- Assessment structure walkthrough: welcome & background, warm-up cardio, dynamic flexibility, fixed weights, free weights, main CV machine, bodyweight exercises, cool-down cardio, static stretches, rest & review
- Full mock attempt and walkthrough of the gym floor assessment
- Pre-requisite: client consultation video assessment completed and passed

---

## Level 3 — Personal Trainer Workshops (2 Hours Each)

### Workshop 3 — PT: Strength & Cardiovascular Programming
- Movement pattern deep dive: squat, hinge, push, pull, lunge and brace
- Assessing movement quality and programming at the right level
- Cardiovascular training programming
- Pre-requisite: all Level 2 modules and practicals completed and passed

### Workshop 4 — PT: Advanced Resistance & Circuits
- Advanced resistance training techniques
- Circuit training design and delivery

### Workshop 5 — PT: Mock Assessment & Final Q&A
- Full practical assessment mock trial
- Open Q&A — your questions, your preparation
