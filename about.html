Our Story

Built by Gym Owners, for the Next Generation of PTs

The Fitness Coach Chronicle was founded with a single belief: that becoming a great personal trainer requires more than memorising anatomy textbooks. It requires learning from people who have actually built successful fitness businesses, retained premium clients, and navigated the real challenges of the industry.

Our tutors have collectively spent over a decade owning and operating gyms in Edinburgh and across Scotland. We've hired PTs, fired PTs, and coached the kind of trainers that clients return to month after month. We built The FCC because we saw a gap — too many newly qualified PTs entering the market with a certificate but no career blueprint.

That's what we fix. Every FCC course is built around getting you qualified, confident, and earning — not just ticking a regulatory box.

Our Credentials & Accreditations

When you graduate from The FCC, you hold qualifications that gym employers across the UK recognise and respect.

CIMSPA Endorsed

The Chartered Institute for the Management of Sport and Physical Activity is the UK's professional body for fitness. CIMSPA endorsement is the gold standard — it's what employers check first.

OFQUAL Regulated

Our Level 3 qualification is regulated by Ofqual, the Office of Qualifications and Examinations Regulation. This means your qualification is nationally recognised and carries real weight with employers.

Active Industry Professionals

Our tutors aren't academics — they're working coaches and gym owners. The FCC meets CIMSPA's standards for tutor industry experience, ensuring you learn from people who are still actively coaching.
