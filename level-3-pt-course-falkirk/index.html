What You'll Learn on the Level 3 PT Course

Our curriculum goes beyond the minimum requirements. Alongside regulated anatomy and programme design modules, you'll cover business, client psychology, and the commercial realities of being a PT.

01

Anatomy & Physiology

Musculoskeletal system, energy systems, biomechanics, and how the body adapts to training stimulus.

02

Exercise Science & Programming

Principles of training, periodisation, progressive overload, and how to design effective programmes for different goals.

03

Client Assessment & Consultation

PAR-Q, lifestyle questionnaires, postural analysis, fitness testing protocols, and how to translate results into action.

04

Nutrition Principles

Evidence-based nutrition for body composition, performance, and health. How to advise clients within your scope of practice.

05

Professional Practice

Insurance, legal responsibilities, duty of care, professional standards, and CIMSPA Code of Conduct.

06

The Career Blueprint

Client acquisition, pricing strategy, social media for PTs, and how to build a client list from your first week qualified.

Becoming a Personal Trainer from Falkirk

From Falkirk, the M9 and M8 motorways provide a clear 50-minute route to Platform Health & Fitness in Leith. It is one of the longer drives to our Edinburgh venue, but for five practical workshop days spread across the full course duration, it is a manageable commitment. All theory is handled online, so the motorway is involved only when you are at the venue.

Falkirk is a Forth Valley town of around 35,000 people with a strong local fitness scene and good transport links in all directions. Newly qualified PTs from Falkirk can build a self-employed client base locally, work at Falkirk's commercial gym facilities, or access Edinburgh's larger market via the motorway network. A CIMSPA-endorsed Level 3 from The FCC is recognised by operators across Scotland.

Falkirk sits between Edinburgh and Stirling, giving graduates access to two substantial fitness markets while remaining based in a town with its own strong demand. The FCC was designed for exactly this kind of student - ambitious, well-located, and wanting a PT qualification that matches the ambition. Five motorway trips for five workshop days, everything else online. The result is a career-ready credential.
