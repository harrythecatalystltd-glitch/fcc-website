Section 9

Content Gap Analysis

Topics competitors rank for that FCC currently has no content targeting.

Complete Edinburgh Career Guide

Owned by: OriGym, PT Academy Content type: Long-form guide (2,000+ words) Difficulty: Low — just needs to be written

"How to become a personal trainer in Edinburgh" is the highest-volume informational query in this niche. Both dominant competitors have pages targeting it. FCC has nothing. A comprehensive guide covering qualifications, cost, duration, Edinburgh salary data, and local gym employment opportunities would rank quickly — and funnel readers directly to FCC's course pages. This is the single highest-impact content gap to close.

What Do Personal Trainers Earn in Edinburgh?

Owned by: OriGym (FAQ) Content type: Blog post / FAQ schema Difficulty: Very low

OriGym answers this as an FAQ. FCC should own this conversation with a dedicated blog section or standalone article. Edinburgh-specific salary data (gym-employed vs. self-employed PT, online vs. in-person rates) is highly compelling for career-change prospects and positions FCC as a credible career advisor, not just a course seller. Great email list bait too.

Local Area Landing Pages (Midlothian, Livingston, Falkirk et al.)

Owned by: Nobody — genuine gap across all competitors Content type: Location landing pages Difficulty: Very low — thin competition

None of the top 5 competitors have well-optimised location pages for the Edinburgh surrounding areas. The Fitness Education Academy covers Penicuik but nothing else beyond Glasgow and Edinburgh. For FCC — a local brand — building location pages for Midlothian, Livingston, Falkirk, East Lothian, West Lothian, and Stirling would generate page-1 rankings almost immediately, as there is virtually no competition for these phrases. Each page needs only ~400 words of location-specific content to rank.

CIMSPA Explained / CIMSPA vs. REPs

Owned by: No Edinburgh-specific competitor Content type: Blog post / explainer Difficulty: Low

Many prospective students don't understand the difference between CIMSPA and the old REPs register, or why CIMSPA accreditation matters to gym employers. An authoritative explainer from FCC — backed by their own CIMSPA credentials — builds trust and ranks for qualification-research queries that are one step before transactional intent. Competitors treat CIMSPA as a badge, not a topic to educate on.

0% Finance / Affordability Content

Owned by: OriGym (mentions it), no one goes deep on it Content type: Pricing / finance explainer page Difficulty: Low

0% finance is mentioned by multiple competitors but none have a dedicated pricing page that explains the finance options, monthly breakdown, and total cost in a transparent, reassuring way. FCC's "0% Finance Plans" are a significant conversion factor. A clear, honest pricing page with a monthly payment calculator or table would rank for cost-intent queries and reduce buyer hesitation significantly.
