What You'll Study on the Online PT Course

The same Level 3 curriculum as any other FCC route — delivered across self-paced e-learning, live lectures, and guided tutor sessions.

01

Anatomy & Physiology

Self-paced e-learning modules covering the musculoskeletal system, energy systems, and how the body adapts to training.

02

Exercise Science & Programming

Principles of training, periodisation, and progressive overload. Design effective programmes for a range of client goals — covered in e-learning and reinforced in live lectures.

03

Client Assessment & Consultation

PAR-Q, postural analysis, fitness testing, and turning results into client programmes — assessed through video practical submissions reviewed by your tutor.

04

Nutrition Principles

Evidence-based nutrition for body composition and performance. How to advise clients within your professional scope.

05

Professional Practice

Insurance, legal responsibilities, duty of care, and CIMSPA Code of Conduct. All delivered and assessed fully online.

06

The Career Blueprint

Client acquisition, pricing strategy, and how to build a PT client list — even before you leave your current job.
